Privacy and Surveillance in AI

Site Author

DigitalOcean

Abstract

On this site by DigitalOcean they explore the ethical problems that come with AI privacy and surveillance. They explain what AI privacy is and the methods used to collect data that have privacy implications. They also emphasize the risks and challenges of AI privacy and surveillance. Overall they are very informative and highlight the importance of knowing what AI privacy is and how we can mitigate the effects.
